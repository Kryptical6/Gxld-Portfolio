Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does the process take?
+

Usually between 2 days and 2 weeks depending on the scale and complexity of the job. Single frames with a clear brief are typically done within 2 to 3 days. Larger UI systems may take longer. I will always give you a clear timeline upfront.

Where can I contact you?
+

The best way to reach me is through Discord. My username is gxld_ofc. You can also click any "Let's Talk" button on this page to jump straight to my profile.

What is importing?
+

Importing is the process of taking a Figma UI design and fully implementing it inside Roblox Studio, including all elements, layout, sizing, fonts, and scripted interactions. Without importing, a design is just a file. Importing makes it live and functional in your game.

Why do you provide the original files?
+

I believe you should fully own what you paid for. The original Figma source files mean you can edit, update, or hand off the design to another developer in the future with no lock-in. It is the professional standard and a mark of quality.

Do you do revisions?
+

Yes. Since you receive daily updates during production, most changes can be made before final delivery. Minor revisions after delivery are included. If requirements change significantly mid-project, we will discuss it fairly.

When do I pay?
+

Payment is after you have reviewed and approved the finished UI. You do not pay until you are happy with the result. Message me on Discord to discuss payment methods that work for you.

Can you match a specific art style or theme?
+

Absolutely. Medieval, futuristic, anime, minimal, western - whatever your game needs. Send references and I will design to match. The more detail you give me upfront, the better the result.
